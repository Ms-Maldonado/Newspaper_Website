Life at Blanson
Genesis ??

Here at Blanson CTE High School, there are numerous clubs offered for you to join; such as Academic Decathlon, BPA, Debate, NAHS, and more! If you don't know where to start, try signing up clubs that capture your interest. By doing so, it gives you an opportunity to meet people who share the same interests as you, which could lead to potential friendships. Though, you don't necessarily have to join clubs to make new friends. Sparking conversations with your classmates or people in your program of study also provides a gateway for developing new bonds. It’s well known that students at Blanson CTE High school are very friendly and encouraging, so don't be afraid to take a risk and strike up conversation. Building connections with your peers is an essential part of the high school experience, and it can lead to lasting friendships and support networks.

Blanson CTE doesn’t just offer fun clubs and opportunities to create friendships, it also offers intriguing extracurricular classes such as Psychology, Architecture Design, or AP Computer Science. Students have the chance to gain certifications by passing their desired certification tests, which not only makes students prepared for the workforce, but also provides a fun environment where students can learn valuable skills that help them in their career path.

With a supportive community and great opportunities, Blanson CTE High School is a phenomenal place to attend and experience the journey of high school, all while preparing for a successful future. So, make the most of your time here, and take advantage of the wonderful experiences and learning opportunities this school has to offer.
